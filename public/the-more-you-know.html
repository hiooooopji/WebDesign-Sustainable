Curious

facts
🌍

Earth's

rotation is slowing

Every century

, Earth's rotation adds about 1.8 milliseconds to a day. In 140 million years, a day will be 25 hours long.

🌌

There are more stars than grains

of sand

Estimates suggest there

are 100-200 billion galaxies in the observable universe, each with millions to billions of stars.

🧠

Your brain generates enough

electricity to power a lightbulb

The 86 billion neurons in your brain produce about 20 watts of electrical power — enough to light a small LED bulb.

🌱

Trees communicate underground

Through networks of fungi

called the "Wood Wide Web," trees share nutrients, send warnings, and support each other.
